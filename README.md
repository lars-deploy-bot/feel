# Pizza Napoletana DOC

## Ingredients

**Dough (48-72h fermentation):**
- 1kg Caputo "00" flour (W280-320)
- 600ml filtered water (60% hydration)
- 20g sea salt from Trapani
- 1g fresh brewer's yeast

**Toppings:**
- DOP San Marzano tomatoes (hand-crushed)
- Mozzarella di Bufala Campana DOP
- Parmigiano-Reggiano DOP (24 months)
- Fresh basil from Genovese variety
- Ligurian extra virgin olive oil
- Fleur de sel

## Method

1. Autolyse flour and water 1 hour
2. Incorporate salt and yeast, mix until windowpane test passes
3. Bulk fermentation 2 hours, fold every 30 minutes
4. Divide into 280g portions
5. Cold fermentation 48-72 hours at 4°C
6. Room temperature 2 hours before shaping
7. Hand-stretch to 35cm diameter, cornicione intact
8. Dress with tomatoes, aged cheese, finish cooking
9. Wood-fired oven 485°C, 60-90 seconds
10. Fresh mozzarella, basil, oil post-bake

The leopard spotting on the crust and perfect char-to-soft ratio distinguishes this from standard pizzeria offerings.
